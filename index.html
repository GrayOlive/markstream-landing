The Problem

Large classes create an assessment crisis.

⏱

Time Consumed

Professors with 200+ students spend 50+ hours marking a single assignment cycle — time stolen from research, teaching, and their own wellbeing.

⚖️

Inconsistent Standards

Marking fatigue introduces grade drift — and larger classes demand more teaching assistants, each with their own interpretation of the rubric. The result is a patchwork of standards where a student's grade can depend more on who marked them than how well they performed.

💬

Feedback Quality Suffers

When pressed for time, feedback becomes generic. Students receive terse comments that don't help them understand or improve their work.

Process

From submission to feedback
in minutes, not weeks.

01

Upload Your Assignment and Rubric

Define your assessment criteria in plain language or import your existing rubric. MarkStream understands nuanced academic standards.

02

Ingest Submissions

Upload student submissions as individual PDFs or bundle them together in a zip file for bulk processing.

03

AI Marks & Explains

Each submission is assessed against your rubric, given a score, and receives specific, constructive feedback grounded in the student's own writing.

04

Review & Release

You review flagged or borderline cases and adjust any grades. When ready, export a CSV or Excel file of all grades and download a zip of annotated PDFs with written feedback for each student.
